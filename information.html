DiPS (Disaster Protecting System) is a groundbreaking initiative, developed by Team Bea Positive, aimed at addressing the growing challenges of disaster management in Bangladesh. Bangladesh is highly vulnerable to natural disasters such as floods, cyclones, and landslides, which frequently disrupt the lives of millions. The country faces immense challenges due to its geographical location and climate change, resulting in frequent and severe natural calamities. DiPS is designed to mitigate the impact of these disasters by offering an advanced, tech-driven solution that helps protect lives and property. The system focuses on real-time monitoring, early warning systems, and efficient resource management, ensuring quicker responses and minimizing the damage caused by these unpredictable events.
